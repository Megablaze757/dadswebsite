About Hastings Build Company

As Hastings Build Company, we provide comprehensive design and build services across Sussex, from initial concept through to final completion. Our commitment is to exceed your expectations by delivering exceptional quality and attention to detail in every project.

With over 30 years of combined experience in the construction industry, we've built our reputation on reliability, craftsmanship, and professional project management. Our fully managed approach ensures a stress-free experience with single-point contact throughout your project.

We operate a transparent fixed-quotation system with no upfront deposit required. Payments are structured against completed work stages, and for larger projects, we engage independent surveyors to provide impartial progress assessments and payment verification.

Trust Hastings Build Company for quality construction and renovation services that stand the test of time. Contact us today for a no-obligation consultation.
