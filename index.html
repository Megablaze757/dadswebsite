About Us

We provide a complete design and build service, from initial design, through to the final finish, our aim to exceed your vision. Having your project fully project managed ensures a minimal stress free process, knowing there is someone who you can deal with on a one to one basis. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail. With 30 years experience in the building industry, our focus is on providing outstanding quality. We operate a detailed fixed quotation system with no initial deposit to commence works and payments only against works completed. With larger projects we appoint an independent surveyor to evaluate completed works for payment. For a stress free, high quality service contact us today for a consultation.

Our Services

Basement Conversion, Complete Bathroom Renovation, Complete Kitchen Renovation, Custom Build Home, Home Renovation, House Extensions, House Restoration, Loft Conversion, New Home Build, Project Management
